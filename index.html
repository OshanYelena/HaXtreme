About The HaXtreme 1.0v

HaXtreme 1.0v is a combination of a workshop series on object-oriented programming(OOP) and a Hackathon at the end of the workshop series.

This competition is inaugurating for the 1st time by the student chapters of ComSoc and Computer Society of IEEE student branch of the University of Ruhuna with the aim of bringing a new dimension to the university competitions.

Enthusiastic undergraduates from any university are welcome to participate in the competition.

The event will be held at the Engineering Faculty premises of the University of Ruhuna, which is situated at Hapugala, Galle.
